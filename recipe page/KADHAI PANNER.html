KADHAI PANNER

Description

Kadhai Paneer is a flavorful North Indian dish made with soft paneer cubes, colorful bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes cooked in a freshly ground kadhai masala. It is known for its rich aroma and slightly spicy taste.

This restaurant-style curry is best enjoyed with naan, roti, paratha, or jeera rice. It is a popular choice for family meals and festive occasions.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil or butter
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp crushed coriander seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil or butter in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Add the onions and sauté until they become translucent.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.
  5. Mix in the coriander powder, red chili powder, turmeric, crushed coriander seeds, garam masala, and salt.
  6. Add the sliced bell peppers and cook for 3–4 minutes.
  7. Add the paneer cubes and gently mix them with the masala.
  8. Cook for another 2–3 minutes, garnish with fresh coriander leaves, and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
